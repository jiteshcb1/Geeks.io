Introduction

In Minutes of Meeting module, an organization is facing the issues while scheduling the meeting, managing of cooperate meeting and organizing them in a proper way. Minutes of Meeting helps to assist the organization, business leaders and team member to connect, collaborate, and stay updated with their work in one platform.In this module, we build solution to help organization to conduct meeting successfully with security and reliability. Minutes of Meeting contains all general and necessary information of organizer and attendees. Minute of Meeting will be used to create a minute of meeting and able to check the attendees in a meeting. This web application will provide all the necessity of meeting and will notify the attendees before meeting has been started. During the situation like COVID-19 pandemic, virtual meeting is an integral part of any organization today and for business continuity .MoM Software contains all general & necessary functions. First User have to registered themselves in the portal in order to get access. Managers, secretary and Meeting attendees will have different set of modules available according to their authorization and task for them. Calling of meeting is the module where agenda of the meeting will be creating in order to distributed to all by the organization. Minute of Meeting would be a module where will be used to create the report of conducted meeting. Finally tracking module where meeting attendees will track the update of completing of their tasks.

Minutes of Meeting (MoM) or Meeting minutes means that written record of the meeting from whatever happened during a meeting. This is important to make because of the decision are taken byorganizer, attendees by number of voting, action taken, agenda of meeting is followed or not and planning of next agenda. So, it will track all what decided in meeting and reanalyze it for all the future decisions too. In MoM, it will include the date, time of meeting, venue, to time, name of the attendees who attended the meeting. It will contain all the necessary acceptance and rejection and new idea, agenda, time, date and other approved data for the next meeting. In this module, the problem solution is that the meeting schedule and agenda of the meeting is informed through mail and before starting of the meeting notification is sent through the software to all the attendees.

Overview

Minutes of Meeting helps you to organize your meeting with proper scheduling and in user-friendly manner with all the security are as follows:

  • Creating and scheduling meeting.
  • To notify to all the attendees with all the basic information of meeting like, objective and purpose of meeting,agenda of meeting, time, venue, date time of the scheduled meet, which will be added to the calendar
  • To circulate and track the minutes of meeting.
  • MoM software helps Organizations and teams to manage number of meetings request to make the process automated & efficient.

Some essential components that we develop as a part of Meeting Management application development focuses to –

  • It sends automated reminders to attendees via email or messages.
  • It shares real-time updates with individual or team on mobile and web platform.
  • It keeps the documents or any other data that has been shared throughout the online sessions secure from malware attacks.
  • Its automatic notifications will ensure updating the team members after any changes are finalized and saved.

This software helps various team across organization to improve meeting effectiveness with an interactive Interface and deliver a feature like pre-meeting preparation, streamlining MoM (Minutes of Meeting) that performs in convenient way and reduce obstacle.

Process Definition

Minutes of Meeting Software Consists of various modules to make the work of organization easier and simple following are those modules:-

  1. Registration and Validation of an organization or Institute.
  2. Creating a User directory.
  3. Calling of meeting.
  4. Circulation.
  5. Tracking.




Registration of an Organization or Institute

Registration and Validation on an organization or Institute
Step-1:- the process starts with the Institute or Organization Registration
Step-2:- If the institute is already registered on the software then using the admin Credentials, he/she directly gets the Admin login page , if not then follow step-3
Step-3:- Register and Validation of the organization or Institute takes place
Step -4:- After validation Admin Id will be generated and then admin can login using its credentials to create user directory
Step-5:- is logged in on the admin page successfully, he/she will get various option like CRUD and Defining

Creating User Directory

Step-1:- IF admin is logged in on the admin page successfully, he/she has to define masters which will be saved on database
Step-2:- after defining masters admin has to create the id for each and every user of organization by setting their roles and credentials
Step-3:-After Creation, admin will publish the credentials of users




Calling of meeting

Step-1:- There will be 2 choose in this module depends on the credentials
First will be admin and second will be user
Step-2:- If admin logins then he/she will create masters and can end the process or can call for the meeting
If user logins then he/she will get the open only for Scheduling of meeting
Step-3:- If the meeting is called by user the he/she has to schedule the meeting then has to define agenda, time and various other information regarding meeting and If the meeting is called by Admin he/she will schedule and call the meeting no approval is required
Step-4:- after the user schedules and call the meeting, it will go for approval by admin If approved then meeting will published and if not then need be change the commented stuffs and resend for approval
Step-5:- After the Publishing the meeting, it will be tracked for the list of attendees

Circulation

Step-1:- Generate Minutes of the meeting and assign tasks and send for the approval by admin
Step-2:- If approved Circulated and if not changes the comment part and send for re approval





Tracking

Step-1:- As soon as the circulation as completed the assigned task will be tracked as per the due date
Step-2:- If the task is completed and all the concerned things are done the process ends and if not the meeting can be called again
